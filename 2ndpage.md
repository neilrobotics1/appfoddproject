# Page 2: Features Overview

## 2nd page
The second page of the website should have the three features listed of the app. 

### Content Requirements
The three features should be listed in **equal-sized boxes** and show that:

1.  **Personalized Food Scanner**
    Fodd can scan your products (item, box, or barcode), and provide an answer based on your specific health profile.
    
2.  **For Everyone**
    Fodd can be used by anyone, from people managing multiple restrictions to people with none.
    
3.  **Recommendation System**
    Fodd has an advanced recommendation system that helps you know what to eat/avoid based on your profile.

### Design Section

The interface should utilize modern graphic design conventions for landing pages, ensuring the layout is visually interesting, sleek, and advanced while clearly conveying information.

#### Styling & Color Palette
* **Base State:** Each box must be **White** with **Black** text.
* **Hover State:** * The box background color must change to **#f3c555**.
    * A **solid Black drop shadow** must appear on the **left side**.
* **Animation:** The transition should include a "pop out" effect where the box and its left-side shadow animate smoothly to create depth.

#### Typography & Visual Aids
* **Font:** Maintain strict consistency with the typography used in the preceding section of the app.
* **Icons:** Each box must include existing, industry-standard icons (e.g., a magnifying glass/barcode for the scanner, a group icon for "For Everyone", and a star/gear for the recommendation system) to serve as visual anchors for the user.

## Layout Execution
* Instead of equal-sized boxes in a grid, the three boxes must be identical in width but stacked vertically in a staggered zigzag pattern.
* **Layout Structure:**
    * **Feature 1:** Box on the Left, Illustration on the Right.
    * **Feature 2:** Box on the Right, Illustration on the Left (below Feature 1).
    * **Feature 3:** Box on the Left, Illustration on the Right (below Feature 2).
* Next to each box, there should be a **highly detailed, realistic UI/vector mock-up** that explicitly demonstrates exactly how the functionality works. Do NOT use abstract, vague, or overly stylized pop-art. 
* The illustrations should be large and prominent (at least `md:h-[400px]`).
* **Animation Context:** When scrolling down the page, explicitly showcase the functionality with a one-time 0.6 second sequence (e.g. throwing an allergy warning, toggling a diet filter, or popping up a recommendation swap).
* Maintain the clean, minimalist aesthetic balancing white space with these realistic application graphics.
